December 2018

Howard Zinn Book Fair

2nd December 2018
City College of San Francisco, Mission Campus, 1125 Valencia Street
San Francisco, CA 94110 United States + Google Map

From the Fair's "about" page: The Howard Zinn Book Fair is an annual celebration of People’s History, Past Present and Future. We gather together authors, zinesters, bloggers and publishers for a day of readings, panel discussions and workshops exploring the value of dissident histories towards building a better future. In the spirit of the late historian Howard Zinn we recognize the stories of the ways that everyday people have risen to propose a world beyond empires big and small.
